How It Started

From a Garage to 50,000+ Happy Customers

ToyVerse was born in a small garage in 2018 when founder Alex Rivera struggled to find high-quality, safe, and imaginative toys for his daughter. Tired of cheap products and overwhelming choices, he decided to curate a collection that met his own high standards.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved brand. Today, ToyVerse ships to over 30 countries and has helped more than 50,000 families find the perfect gift — from STEM-focused building kits to classic plushies that stand the test of time.

Every product in our catalogue is hand-picked by our expert team, tested for safety, and reviewed by real parents and children before it reaches your doorstep.
